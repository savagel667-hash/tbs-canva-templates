BOOKING POLICIES
CANCELLATION
Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations forfeit 50% of the service price.
INTAKE & PHOTOS
First-time guests complete a skin-history intake and consent to before/after photos kept private to your file.
CONTRAINDICATIONS
Active Accutane, recent retinol, pregnancy, and sunburn require disclosure 24h before — we may need to modify your treatment.
LATE ARRIVAL
After a 15-minute grace window we may need to shorten or reschedule your session.
