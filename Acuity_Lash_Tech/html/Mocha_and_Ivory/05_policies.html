BOOKING POLICIES
CANCELLATION
Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations forfeit 50% of the service price.
ALLERGY & PATCH TEST
First-time clients require a patch test 48 hours before. We do not service active eye infections or styes.
DEPOSITS
A non-refundable 25% deposit secures all full sets. Applied to your final total.
LATE ARRIVAL
After a 15-minute grace window we may need to convert your full set into a fill or reschedule.
